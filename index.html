Deirdre Nicholls

About Me

Contemporary artist working with various mediums to explore the intersection of nature and human experience. My work spans sculpture, painting, and mixed media installations, drawing inspiration from the organic patterns and rhythms found in nature. Through my art, I seek to create immersive experiences that invite viewers to reflect on their connection to the natural world and each other.

I regularly exhibit my work in galleries and collaborate with other artists on environmental art projects. When I'm not creating, I enjoy sharing my knowledge through workshops and mentoring emerging artists.
